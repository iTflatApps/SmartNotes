Privacy Policy

  1. The term "personal information", as used herein, is defined as any information that identifies or can be used to identify, contact or locate the person to whom such information relates. The personal information we collect will be the subject of this privacy policy, as amended from time to time.
  2. We do not ask for any information about you.
  3. Smart Notes app do not sell any content.
  4. Smart Notes app do not require registration.
  5. The security of your personal information will not be compromised.
  6. Smart Notes app do not ask for location information through our mobile app.
  7. Smart Notes app do not send news to email.

  8. Required Permissions:

    1. "CAMERA" permission is used to create note's photos and scan QR codes.
    2. "READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE" permission is used to attach photos from gallery.
    3. "ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY" permission is used to send notifications from app.
    4. "VIBRATE" permission is used to notify user about wrong password.
    5. "USE_BIOMETRIC" permission is used for fast app login with fingerprint.
    6. "SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW" permission is used to display the application in full screen, this permission does not affect the visibility of the system views.
    7. "QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES" permission is used for the "attach application" function, the Smart Notes app does not collect any information about the installed applications on your device.
    8. "RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED" permission is used to restore the normal operation of reminders after a reboot.
